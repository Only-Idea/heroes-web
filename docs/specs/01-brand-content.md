# Brand Overview & Content Inventory

## What is Heroes?

Heroes is a purpose-driven virtual challenge platform. Users participate in virtual challenges themed around iconic Japanese routes — Mt. Fuji, the 47 Ronin trail, and Japanese railways — turning fitness into environmental impact. Available on iOS and Android (Flutter), supporting 32+ countries, 12 activity types (including wheelchair), with challenges spanning 1–52 weeks.

## Challenge Routes

| Challenge | Theme | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Fujisan (富士山) | Mt. Fuji Summit | Yoshida trail to the summit with real mountain hut landmarks |
| Ronin (浪人) | 47 Ronin / Chushingura | Historic loyalty trail through culturally significant locations |
| Railway (鉄道) | Japanese Railways | Famous rail lines with real train station landmarks |

## Six Messaging Pillars

1. 「すべての一歩にインパクトを」 — Every step creates real environmental impact (tree planting, ocean cleanup)
2. 「本物を手に入れる」 — Premium physical medal shipped worldwide upon 100% completion
3. 「一歩ずつ日本を探検」 — Discover landmarks through interactive Mapbox maps (3 styles)
4. 「ヒーローになる」 — Achievements across 10 categories, 5 rarities (Common to Legendary)
5. 「自分のペースで動く」 — 12 activity types, flexible 1–52 week timeframes
6. 「一緒にもっと強く」 — Team features with real-time chat, leaderboards, community feed

## Environmental Impact

## Key Features for Website Content

- Interactive Mapbox maps (Classic / Night / Satellite styles)
- Real-time participant tracking on map
- Team functionality with real-time chat and shared progress
- Achievement system: 10 categories, 5 rarity tiers
- Streak tracking for consecutive activity days
- Community feed with posts, likes, comments
- 12 activity types including wheelchair accessibility
- Premium physical medal with original packaging — shipped worldwide

## Brand Voice

- **Empowering**: Celebrate the inner hero, never preach
- **Culturally respectful**: Japan is depicted with accuracy and reverence
- **Warm and inclusive**: All fitness levels and abilities welcome
- **Premium but accessible**: Reflect quality without exclusivity
- **Adventure-oriented**: Frame as 旅 (journey) and 冒険 (adventure), never トレーニング (training)
- **Purpose-driven**: Always connect fitness to environmental and social impact
